How to tag a milestone

Add @milestone or @milestone:category to any line in any note. Categories: career, business, health, learning, finance, personal.

Examples:
- [x] Landed first enterprise client @milestone:business
- [x] Ran first 5K @milestone:health
Got promoted to senior engineer @milestone:career
